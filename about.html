The History of Tahir

The Heritage of Excellence

We are by reputation, the most prestigious, elegant and luxurious hotel in the north of Nigeria. Tahir Guest Palace Hotel, founded in 1994, has been considered an icon for hotels to follow. Since our opening, we have provided first class accommodation to the people of Nigeria, holiday makers and important dignitaries and business people from around the world.

Initially opened as Tahir Guest Inn when our Chairman Mr. Tahir Fadlallah decided that the city of Kano did not have a suitable hotel to accommodate its people or its visitors. He had a vision; to offer the people of Kano excellent accommodation and services. With a focus on luxury, space, comfort, good value, customer services and maintenance. Today we have over 300 rooms.

Here at Tahir Guest Palace, we take pride in our accommodation and make sure that after you check out, your definition of luxury would change. Our rooms are spacious and well equipped with 24 hour room service, luxurious bathrooms, climate control, over 30 channels satellite digital TV, Fridge and complimentary tea/coffee items. Also, to further improve our security and efficiency of operation, our doors are now equipped with electronic locking systems which operate using key cards. With top class furniture created by our own standby furnishing factory, a choice of carpet, tiles or granite floors, and 24 hours electricity and water supply (via backup generators and storage tanks), you would never want to leave.
